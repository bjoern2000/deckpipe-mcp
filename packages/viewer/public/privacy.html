privacy policy

last updated: march 30, 2026

who operates this

deckpipe is operated by Björn Schefzyk, Germany. Contact: bjoern.schefzyk@gmail.com

what I collect

Deck content you submit via the API (slides, titles, images). Standard server logs (IP address, timestamps, user agent) for operational purposes. That's it. No cookies, no tracking scripts, no analytics.

what I don't collect

No personal accounts, no email addresses, no payment info. deckpipe has no authentication system — decks are accessed by their unique ID.

no authentication

deckpipe does not currently offer authentication or password protection. Any deck is accessible to anyone who has its URL. Do not store sensitive, confidential, or personal information in your decks. You are solely responsible for who you share deck URLs with.

how your data is used

Deck content is stored to render and serve your slides. Server logs are used to debug issues and prevent abuse. I don't sell, share, or use your data for anything else.

where data is stored

Data is stored on servers in the EU (Railway). Uploaded images are stored on disk alongside the application.

data retention

Decks persist until you delete them via the API. I may remove decks after extended periods of inactivity. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days.

your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since there are no accounts, contact me with your deck IDs and I'll handle it. Email: bjoern.schefzyk@gmail.com

changes

I may update this policy. Check back here for the latest version.
